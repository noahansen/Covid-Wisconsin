Description

This plot compares the 20 counties with the highest number of positive cases of Covid-19. The percentage of positive cases out of all of the tested patients is shown, as well as the percentage of deaths out of those positive cases, comparing the rate of infection across counties in Wisconsin. Populated counties, such as Milwaukee and Dane, show different trends, as Milwaukee has a 12.4% difference between infection and deaths, while Dane only has a 0.6% difference. This suggests a difference in practices dealing with limiting the disease, as Dane has 13.8% lower infection rate than Milwaukee.
