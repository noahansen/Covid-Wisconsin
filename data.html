Data

We compiled multiple data sets into one cumulative csv file that maps all of the counties in Wisconsin to different statistics and information regarding Covid-19. This data set can be found in final.zip titled as 'final_data.csv'.

Final_Data Columns Explained

  • Positive, Negative, Deaths

    • Positive and negative add up to the total tests column, the amount of tests that either turned up positive or negative. Deaths is as it sounds, but is a subsection of the positive tests, and not it's own count.

  • Total Tests

    • Positive + negative tests.

  • Percent Positive

    • Percent of all administered tests that turned up positive ie) positive/total tests

  • Percent Deaths

    • Percent of all positive tests that resulted in deaths ie) positive/deaths

  • Geometry

    • Used to create maps of the county boundaries

  • Population

    • Population of each county

  • Percent pop pos

    • Percentage of the total population that has been infected ie) positive/population

  • Percent pop tested

    • Percentage of the total population that has been tested ie) total tests/population

  • Percent pop dead

    • Percentage of the total population who has died ie) population/deaths
